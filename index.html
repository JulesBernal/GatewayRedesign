Edmonton, Alberta

Edmonton is the capital of the Alberta Province of Canada. It is home to 1 million residents. The city sits on the North Saskatchwean River. It is nickanmed "Canada's Festival City" for hosting festivals all throughout the year.

Fun fact: Edmonton has a sports rivalry with Calgary, another city in Alberta. The rivalry is so intense, it has been dubbed the battle of Alberta.
