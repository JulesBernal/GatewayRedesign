Attractions of Edmonton

West Edmonton Mall

Largest mall in Canada, and second largest in North America. Features over 800 stores, attractions, hotels, and restaurants. Home to the largest indoor waterpark, and Fantasyland Hotel, fun themed hotel rooms.

Suitable Demographic: All ages

Cost of Entry: Free

8882 170 St NW, Edmonton, AB T5T 4J2, Canada

Art Galley of Alberta

Art museum featuring eight temporary exhibits and permanent exhibits of various Artists such as Maud Lewis and Byron Samson.

Suitable Demographic: All ages

Cost of Entry: Free to paid admission of max $14 CAD

2 Sir Winston Churchill Sq, Edmonton, AB T5J 2C1, Canada



Muttart Conservatory

Largest indoor botanical gardens in canada, with events and courses.

Suitable Demographic: All ages

Cost of Entry: Free to paid admission of max $15 CAD

9626 96a St NW, Edmonton, AB T6C 4L8, Canada



Edmonton Valley Zoo

Zoo situted on Alberta's river valley, opened 364 days of the year, home to more than 350 animals, offering fun for the whole family.

Suitable Demographic: All ages

Cost of Entry: Free to paid admission of max $11 CAD

13315 Buena Vista Road &, 87 Ave NW, Edmonton, AB T5J 2R7, Canada



The Alberta legislature Building

Dubbed the "ledge" or "the leg" by locals, features a park with acoustics.

Suitable Demographic: All ages

Cost of Entry: Free to public as well as for Tours

10800 97 Ave NW, Edmonton, AB T5K 2B6, Canada
